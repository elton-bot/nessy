## Defects found & fixed during QA
1. **Timezone off-by-one (critical).** Dates were written with `toISOString()`
   (UTC) but compared at local midnight, so on a UTC+ machine every due/expiry
   date shifted one day (AC showed "6d overdue" for a 5-day-overdue item;
   completed daily task showed "next Today"). **Fix:** introduced
   `src/domain/dates.ts` (local-component format/parse) and routed seed, store,
   selectors, Tasks and Inventory through it. Re-verified: AC now "5d overdue",
   reschedules correct.
